Frequently Asked Questions

What exactly is customized jewellery?

Customized jewellery is any piece of jewellery that is created specifically to suit your individual taste and specific requirement. A lot of our clients share their ideas and designs with us and explain in detail their specifications. The end jewellery is made keeping their needs in mind. We also consult our clients on what kind of customization would suit them the best.

What karatage of gold is used in the jewellery and is it certified?

We use 18kt and 22kt gold depending on the type of jewellery being made. The jewellery comes with the BIS Hallmarking certifying the purity of the gold used.

Are the diamonds certified?

All diamonds above 0.30 cents (each stone) comes with the international GIA certificate which globally accepted. Diamonds below 0.30 cents come along with details of the total,no of diamonds used along with its weight.

Can we give our old gold and get new jewellery made?

Yes. When you give us your old jewellery, we first melt and purify the same and let you know the available gold. The purified gold is then used in making the new jewellery.

What is your return policy?

Given that we create customized pieces of jewellery, the piece is very unique to your requirements. However, we value your intent of return, and can make use of the jewellery (melt the gold, re-use diamonds) in creating a new piece of you. The point of deduction would be just the making charge. Unlike retail, we refrain from buying back jewellery and reselling the same.

How can I place an order?

a. Call the number on the website, discuss your requirement and place the order via call.
b. Drop an email and place the order.
c. Request for an in-person consultation and place your order.

Do you source your jewellery or manufacture it yourself?

We have our own manufacturing unit in Bangalore and our jewellery is mostly handmade.

Do you deliver across India?

Yes, we do deliver our products all across India. A nominal delivery charge will be borne by you to receive the jewellery.

What is the estimated time taken to make a jewellery?

We normally take around a week to 10days of time to make basic jewellery. Depending on the design and kind of work , we might sometimes take a little more. We however, always keep you posted on the delivery timelines and also ensure the desired delivery date is met.

What are the payment timelines?

50% advance to be made at the time of order booking and 50% at the time of order delivery.
