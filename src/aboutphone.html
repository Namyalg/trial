OUR STORY

Every creation has its own story. Creating jewellery, that reveals intricate craftsmanship while being playful and innovative while offering a unique and surreal aesthetic; catering to the modern woman's desire for jewellery to be part of her personal identity.


Thirtha spent a decade working with large multinational companies and the Big 4’s, spread across a variety of verticals which included strategic management, process improvements, and developing Quality Management Systems to name a few; before realizing her true calling for conceptualizing and creating precious jewellery.

Her unique design sensibility represents the best of many worlds, and her creations are a delight to wear.

She took on a variety of roles at work and parallelly took on select projects of bespoke jewellery, just as they had envisioned it at work and piloted her entrepreneurial idea while still at corporate, finally deciding to take the plunge, only to find out that her startup would set a benchmark and impact people's lives daintly.


She is driven by a passion for designing works of art through jewellery that recognises individual style. Her unique design sensibility represents the best of many worlds, and her creations are a delight to wear. Samaara Jewellery is also an IIM Bangalore NSRCEL pre-incubated venture focused on creating customized jewellery. Thirtha is a lifelong learner and has a Bachelor’s Degree in Commerce, Post Graduate Diploma in International Business, a Diploma in Jewellery Designing and still learning.


Today, she single-handedly runs and manages her jewellery startup, spends time influencing and nurturing young entrepreneurial minds through focused sessions and talks while also being an adoring mother of two amazing children.
